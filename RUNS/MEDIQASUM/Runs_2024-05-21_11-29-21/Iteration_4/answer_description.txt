1. Assessment: Provide a thorough evaluation of the patient's current status and condition.
2. Assessment and Plan: Document the diagnosis and proposed treatment plan based on the assessment.
3. Birth History (if applicable): Record relevant information about the patient's delivery, including gestational age, labor and delivery complications, and neonatal outcomes.
4. Chief Complaint: State the reason the patient sought medical attention.
5. History of Present Illness: Detail the current symptoms and their development.
6. Medical History: Document past illnesses, allergies, surgeries, and family history.
7. Medications: List current and previous medications, dosages, and durations.
8. Physical Examination: Document findings from the examination of various body systems.
9. Plan: Outline the proposed treatment plan, including interventions, follow-up appointments, and patient education.

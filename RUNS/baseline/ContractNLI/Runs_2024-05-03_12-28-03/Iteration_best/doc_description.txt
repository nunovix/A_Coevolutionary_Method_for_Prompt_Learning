This instruction concerns the conditions of a Non-Disclosure Agreement (NDA):

1. The recipient agrees to keep confidential any proprietary information disclosed.
2. Unauthorized disclosure is prohibited.
3. Exceptions to confidentiality include information that is already known or that becomes publicly available.
4. The NDA remains in effect for a specified period of time.
5. Breach of the NDA may result in legal consequences.

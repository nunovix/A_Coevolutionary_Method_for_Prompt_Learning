This instruction pertains to the conditions of a Non-Disclosure Agreement (NDA): 

1. The receiving party agrees to keep confidential any and all information disclosed by the disclosing party.
2. The confidential information may only be used for the purpose of performing under the NDA.
3. The confidential information shall be returned or destroyed upon termination or expiration of the NDA.
4. The receiving party shall restrict access to the confidential information to authorized personnel only.
5. The receiving party shall implement reasonable security measures to protect the confidential information.
6. The NDA shall remain in effect for a specified period of time.
7. Breach of the NDA may result in legal consequences.

Each comprehensive CTR comprises four distinct sections: (1) INCLUSION CRITERIA, outlining the conditions for patient eligibility, (2) THERAPEUTIC INTERVENTION, providing specifics on treatment type, dosage, administration frequency, and treatment length, (3) PARTICIPANT OUTCOMES, summarizing participant demographics, measured outcomes, units of measurement, and study conclusions, and (4) ENCOUNTERED ADVERSE EVENTS, documenting any observed adverse signs and symptoms.

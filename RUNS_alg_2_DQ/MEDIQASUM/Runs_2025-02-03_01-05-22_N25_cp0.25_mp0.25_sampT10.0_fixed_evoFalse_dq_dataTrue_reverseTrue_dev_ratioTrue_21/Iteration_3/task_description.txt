1. HISTORY OF PRESENT ILLNESS:
   - Record the patient's chief complaints, including onset, chronology, quality, and severity.
   - Document measurable and objective facts, such as vital signs and findings.

2. PHYSICAL EXAM:
   - Describe the patient's physical examination, including any notable findings.

3. RESULTS:
   - Record the results of diagnostic tests, investigations, and other relevant data.

4. ASSESSMENT AND PLAN:
   - Document the doctor's interpretation, including a list of diagnoses with relevant attributes.
   - Outline the treatment plan, including necessary investigations, referrals, medications, and therapies.

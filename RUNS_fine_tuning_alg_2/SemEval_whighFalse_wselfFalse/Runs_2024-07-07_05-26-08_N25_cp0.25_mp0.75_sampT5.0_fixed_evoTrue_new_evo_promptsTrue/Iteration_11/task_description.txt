These reports are divided into four distinct parts: (1) ELIGIBILITY CRITERIA, which outlines the criteria for patient inclusion, (2) INTERVENTION, detailing the treatment specifics such as type, dosage, frequency, and duration, (3) RESULTS, summarizing participant demographics, outcome metrics, units of measurement, and final conclusions, and (4) ADVERSE EVENTS, documenting any unforeseen adverse reactions.

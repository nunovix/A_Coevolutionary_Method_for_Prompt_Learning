Prepare a thorough clinical note, encompassing the following segments:

1. Assessment: Present condition and assessment of the patient's health.
2. Assessment and Plan: Identification of the patient's problem and proposed treatment.
3. Birth History (if applicable): Background information about the patient's delivery.
4. Chief Complaint: Description of the reason for the patient's visit.
5. History of Present Illness: Current symptoms and their development.
6. Medical History: Previous medical conditions and treatments.
7. Medications: Current and past prescription medications.
8. Physical Examination: Findings from the physical assessment of the patient.
9. Plan: Recommendations for further diagnostic tests, treatments, or follow-up care.

Ensure each segment is fully detailed and accurate.

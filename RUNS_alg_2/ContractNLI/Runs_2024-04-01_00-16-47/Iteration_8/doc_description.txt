This instruction outlines the confidentiality terms of a Non-Disclosure Agreement (NDA):

1. The receiving party shall keep secret and protect from unauthorized disclosure all confidential information disclosed by the disclosing party.
2. The receiving party shall restrict access to the confidential information to only those employees or third parties who have a need to know.
3. The receiving party shall use the confidential information only for the purpose of carrying out its obligations under the NDA.
4. The receiving party shall implement reasonable security measures to safeguard the confidential information.
5. The confidentiality obligations shall survive the termination of the NDA.

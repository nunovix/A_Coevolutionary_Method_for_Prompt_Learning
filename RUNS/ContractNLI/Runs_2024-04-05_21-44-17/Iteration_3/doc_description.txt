The following sections outline the terms of a Non-Disclosure Agreement (NDA). This agreement confirms that both parties involved agree to keep confidential any proprietary or sensitive information exchanged during business discussions. The NDA sets forth the duration of the agreement, any exceptions to the confidentiality obligation, and the consequences for breaching the agreement.
----------


A Non-Disclosure Agreement (NDA) typically includes the following sections:

1. Confidentiality obligations defining the protected information and the parties' obligations to maintain its confidentiality.
2. Exclusions from confidential information, specifying what information is not protected under the agreement.
3. Duration and termination of the agreement, detailing how long the agreement is binding and the conditions for its termination.
4. Consequences for breach of the agreement, outlining the remedies available in case of a violation.

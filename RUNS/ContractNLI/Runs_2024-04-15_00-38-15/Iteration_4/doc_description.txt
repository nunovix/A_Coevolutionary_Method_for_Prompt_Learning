The following instruction outlines the key elements of a Non-Disclosure Agreement (NDA):

1. Confidentiality: Parties agree to keep confidential all information shared under the NDA, which encompasses data not previously known, unmarked as confidential, or reasonably inferable as confidential.
2. Exceptions: Confidentiality obligations do not extend to disclosures made with consent or as mandated by law.
3. Duration: Confidentiality obligations persist for a defined timeframe or until agreement termination.
4. Consequences: Violations may result in legal action.
5. Return/Destruction: Upon request or agreement termination, parties must return or destroy all Confidential Information.
6. Legal Framework: The NDA is subject to the specified law and jurisdiction.

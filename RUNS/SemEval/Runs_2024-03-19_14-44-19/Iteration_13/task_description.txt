A complete CTR is structured into four sections: (1) Eligibility Criteria defining patient inclusion conditions, (2) Intervention detailing treatment type, dosage, frequency, and duration, (3) Results summarizing participant data, outcome measures, units, and conclusions, and (4) Adverse Events recording observed signs and symptoms.

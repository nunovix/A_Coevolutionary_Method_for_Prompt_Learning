2->
A Clinical Trial Report must contain a standard section for the following descriptions:

1. Study Design: Outline of the experimental plan, including type of study (interventional, observational), randomization method, and blinding.
2. Methods: Description of the participant recruitment, sample size, data collection procedures, and statistical analysis.
3. Results: Presentation of the data, including demographics, outcomes, and statistical analysis.
4. Discussion: Interpretation of the findings, comparison with previous studies, and implications for future research.
5. Conclusion: Summary of the study's main findings and clinical significance.
6. References: List of cited sources used in the report.
----------
3->
The report of a clinical trial features a dedicated section for presenting the trial's data.
----------
4->
The descriptions refer to sections of Clinical Trial Reports (CTRs), specifically the Background and Objectives, Methods, Results, or Discussion components.

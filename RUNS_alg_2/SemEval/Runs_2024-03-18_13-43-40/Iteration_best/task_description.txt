

Assess the alignment and logical consistency of clinical domain experts' statements with Clinical Trial Reports (CTRs). CTRs serve to detail the methodologies and findings of clinical trials, evaluating treatment efficacy and safety. Experts' statements must be substantiated by the CTR data and not contradict reported descriptions.

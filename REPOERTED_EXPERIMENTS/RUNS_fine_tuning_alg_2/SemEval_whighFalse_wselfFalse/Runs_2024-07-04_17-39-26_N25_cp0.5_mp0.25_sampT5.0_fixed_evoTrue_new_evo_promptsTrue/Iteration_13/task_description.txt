CTRs are systematically divided into four key sections: (1) ELIGIBILITY CRITERIA detailing the criteria for patient inclusion, (2) INTERVENTION outlining the treatment specifics such as type, dosage, frequency, and duration, (3) RESULTS presenting participant data, outcome measures, and conclusions, and (4) ADVERSE EVENTS documenting any unforeseen adverse occurrences.

CTRs consist of four main sections: Eligibility Criteria, Intervention, Results, and Adverse Events. Clinical statements make claims about information in these sections, either for a single CTR or for comparisons between two CTRs. Examine the key components of CTRs to assess the evidence supporting clinical assertions, where clinical assertions are statements that make claims about the information in these sections, either within a single report or across comparisons between two reports.

CTRs encapsulate trial protocols and results for assessing new therapies' effectiveness and safety, encompassing patient groups subjected to different treatments or with varying initial characteristics. CTRs are segmented into four key sections: (1) Patient Selection Criteria, (2) Treatment Specifications (type, dosage, frequency, duration), (3) Summary of Findings (participant numbers, outcome metrics, units, conclusions), and (4) Recorded Adverse Reactions (symptoms noted during the trial). Ensure expert commentary is evidence-based, aligns with the CTR data, and remains consistent with the report's findings.

1->Assess the semantic entailment connections between distinct sections of Clinical Trial Reports (CTRs) and statements made by clinical domain experts. To establish entailment, statements must be grounded in the CTR information and not contradict any of the provided descriptions. The CTRs comprise four key sections: 

1. ELIGIBILITY CRITERIA: specifies the conditions for patient inclusion. 
2. INTERVENTION: details treatments from different patient cohorts. 
3. RESULTS: summarizes participant statistics and trial outcomes. 
4. ADVERSE EVENTS: lists observed signs and symptoms. 

Statements make claims about the information within these sections, either for a single CTR or for comparisons between two CTRs. Ensure that statements are supported by the CTR information and do not contradict any descriptions.

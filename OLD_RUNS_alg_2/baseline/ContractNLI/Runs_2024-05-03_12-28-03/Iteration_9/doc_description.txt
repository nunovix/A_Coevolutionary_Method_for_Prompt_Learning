This instruction outlines the conditions of a Non-Disclosure Agreement (NDA): 

1. The receiving party agrees to keep confidential any information disclosed by the disclosing party.
2. The confidential information may be used solely for the purpose of carrying out the business relationship between the parties.
3. The receiving party shall restrict access to the confidential information to those employees or third parties who have a need to know.
4. The receiving party shall implement reasonable measures to protect the confidential information from unauthorized disclosure.
5. The obligations of confidentiality shall survive the termination of the business relationship.
6. The receiving party may disclose the confidential information only with the prior written consent of the disclosing party or as required by law.
7. The receiving party acknowledges that any unauthorized disclosure of confidential information may cause irreparable harm to the disclosing party.

This instruction pertains to the conditions of a Non-Disclosure Agreement (NDA): 

1. The receiving party agrees to keep confidential any and all information disclosed by the disclosing party.
2. The confidential information may only be used for the purpose of performing under the NDA.
3. The confidential information shall be returned or destroyed upon termination or expiration of the NDA.
4. The receiving party shall restrict access to the confidential information to authorized personnel only.
5. The receiving party shall implement reasonable security measures to protect the confidential information.
6. The NDA shall remain in effect for a specified period of time.
7. Breach of the NDA may result in legal consequences.

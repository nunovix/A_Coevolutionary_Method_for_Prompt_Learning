A thorough CTR includes four critical components: (1) ELIGIBILITY CRITERIA, specifying the qualifications required for trial participation, (2) INTERVENTION, detailing the treatment specifics such as type, dosage, frequency, and duration, (3) RESULTS, summarizing the number of participants, outcome measures, units, and conclusions, and (4) ADVERSE EVENTS, documenting any symptoms observed in participants during the trial.

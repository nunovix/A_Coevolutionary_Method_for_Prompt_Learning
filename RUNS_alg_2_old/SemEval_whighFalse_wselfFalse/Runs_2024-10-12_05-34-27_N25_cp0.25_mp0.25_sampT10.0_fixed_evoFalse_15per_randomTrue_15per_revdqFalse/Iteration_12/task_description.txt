0->Analyze Clinical Trial Reports (CTRs) to evaluate the validity of clinical statements regarding patient eligibility, intervention specifics, results, and adverse events. Each CTR is divided into four key sections: (1) ELIGIBILITY CRITERIA, which outlines the inclusion and exclusion criteria for patient participation, (2) INTERVENTION, detailing the treatment's type, dosage, frequency, and duration, (3) RESULTS, summarizing participant demographics, outcome measures, units of measurement, and final conclusions, and (4) ADVERSE EVENTS, documenting any unexpected signs or symptoms observed during the trial. Assess the accuracy and relevance of claims made about these sections, whether they pertain to a single CTR or involve a comparative analysis between two.

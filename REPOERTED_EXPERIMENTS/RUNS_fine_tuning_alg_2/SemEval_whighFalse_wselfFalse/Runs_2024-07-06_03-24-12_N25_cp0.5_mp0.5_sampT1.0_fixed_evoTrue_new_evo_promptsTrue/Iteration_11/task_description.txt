These reports are divided into four critical components: (1) ELIGIBILITY CRITERIA, which specify the inclusion criteria for patient selection, (2) INTERVENTION, which describes the treatment administered, including type, dosage, frequency, and duration, (3) RESULTS, which provide a summary of participant characteristics, outcome measures, units of measurement, and final conclusions, and (4) ADVERSE EVENTS, which note any unexpected adverse incidents.

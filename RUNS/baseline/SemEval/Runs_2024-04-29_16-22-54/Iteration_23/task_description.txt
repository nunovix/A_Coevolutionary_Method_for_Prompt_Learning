Ensure consistency between professionals' statements and CTR data, avoiding discrepancies. CTRs include comprehensive information on clinical investigations, covering trial methods, findings, and safety and efficacy data. Key components of a fully documented CTR are:

1. Patient Eligibility Criteria: outlined selection guidelines
2. Intervention Details: procedure, dosage, frequency, and duration
3. Evaluation Measures: patient demographics, diagnostic procedures, and trial outcomes
4. Adverse Events: documented side effects and symptoms.

Clinical experts may refer to individual CTRs or compare multiple reports for an in-depth assessment.

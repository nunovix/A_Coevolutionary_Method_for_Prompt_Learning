CTRs represent detailed documents outlining clinical trials for novel treatments, comprising multiple cohorts or arms, with distinct treatment methods, dosages, frequencies, durations, and patient profiles. A fully-realized CTR encompasses four primary sections: (1) ELIGIBILITY CRITERIA, detailing patient eligibility requirements, (2) INTERVENTION, defining treatment protocols, (3) RESULTS, providing demographic information, outcome measures, and conclusions, and (4) ADVERSE EVENTS, recording observed side effects.

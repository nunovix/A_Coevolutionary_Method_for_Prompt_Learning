These reports are segmented into four distinct parts: (1) ELIGIBILITY CRITERIA, which outlines the criteria for patient inclusion, (2) INTERVENTION, detailing the treatment specifics such as type, dosage, frequency, and duration, (3) RESULTS, summarizing the participant demographics, outcome metrics, units of measurement, and the study's conclusions, and (4) ADVERSE EVENTS, documenting any unanticipated adverse reactions.

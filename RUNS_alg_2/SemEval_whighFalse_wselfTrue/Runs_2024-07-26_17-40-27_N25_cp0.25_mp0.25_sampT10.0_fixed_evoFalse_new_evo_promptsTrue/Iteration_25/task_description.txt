A thorough CTR is segmented into four principal divisions: (1) ELIGIBILITY CRITERIA, which specifies the prerequisites for patient inclusion, (2) INTERVENTION, detailing the nature, dosage, frequency, and duration of the treatments administered, (3) RESULTS, which encapsulates the number of participants, the metrics used for measuring outcomes, the units of measurement, and the derived conclusions, and (4) ADVERSE EVENTS, cataloging any adverse symptoms experienced by participants during the trial.

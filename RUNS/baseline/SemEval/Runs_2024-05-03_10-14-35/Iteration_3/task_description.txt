Clinical Trial Reports encompass experimental designs and findings from trials assessing the effectiveness and safety of new treatments. A trial typically comprises 1-2 patient groups, or cohorts/arms, distinguished by treatment methods or patient characteristics. The CTR framework includes:

1. Enrollment Criteria: specifying conditions for patient selection.
2. Intervention Description: outlining treatment details, including type, dosage, frequency, and duration.
3. Outcomes: reporting participant numbers, measures, units, and conclusions.
4. Adverse Effects: documenting observed patient symptoms.

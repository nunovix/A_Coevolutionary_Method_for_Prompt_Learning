Participants are grouped into distinct cohorts or 'arms', each subjected to varied treatments or characterized by different baseline attributes. A comprehensive CTR comprises four critical elements: (1) ELIGIBILITY CRITERIA delineating the criteria for patient selection, (2) INTERVENTION details specifying the treatment type, dosage, frequency, and duration, (3) RESULTS reporting the participant count, outcome metrics, measurement units, and inferred conclusions, and (4) ADVERSE EVENTS cataloging any adverse symptoms reported by participants during the study.

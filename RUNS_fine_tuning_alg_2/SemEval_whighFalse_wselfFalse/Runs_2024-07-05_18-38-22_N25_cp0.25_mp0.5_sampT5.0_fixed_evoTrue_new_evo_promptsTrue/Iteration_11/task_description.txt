CTRs document the design and results of clinical trials assessing new treatments' effectiveness and safety. These reports are divided into four critical components: (1) ELIGIBILITY CRITERIA, specifying the inclusion criteria for patients, (2) INTERVENTION, describing the treatment specifics such as type, dosage, frequency, and duration, (3) RESULTS, presenting participant demographics, outcome measures, units of measurement, and conclusions, and (4) ADVERSE EVENTS, recording any unexpected adverse reactions.

Nondisclosure Agreement (NDA):

1. Maintain secrecy regarding disclosed information.
2. Avoid unauthorized dissemination.
3. Exceptions: Information previously known or publicly available.
4. Timebound: Validity period specified in agreement.
5. Penalties: Potential for legal repercussions for violation.

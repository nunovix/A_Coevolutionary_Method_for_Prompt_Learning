Each trial comprises 1-2 patient cohorts with varying treatments or baseline characteristics. CTRs are divided into four sections: (1) ELIGIBILITY CRITERIA, outlining conditions for patient participation, (2) INTERVENTION, detailing treatment type, dosage, frequency, and duration, (3) RESULTS, summarizing participant count, outcome measures, units, and conclusions, and (4) ADVERSE EVENTS, listing observed signs and symptoms in patients. Statements made by clinical experts claim information from these sections, either for a single CTR or when comparing two. Establish entailment by ensuring the claim is related to the CTR content, supported by the CTR, and consistent with the provided descriptions.

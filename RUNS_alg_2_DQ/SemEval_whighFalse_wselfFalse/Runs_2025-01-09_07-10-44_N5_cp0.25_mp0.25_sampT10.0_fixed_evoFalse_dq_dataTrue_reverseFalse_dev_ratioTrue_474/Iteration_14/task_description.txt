0->Analyze individual sections of Clinical Trial Reports (CTRs) to determine the support for clinical statements. CTRs consist of four main sections: Eligibility Criteria, Intervention, Results, and Adverse Events. Clinical statements make claims about information in these sections, either for a single CTR or for comparisons between two CTRs.

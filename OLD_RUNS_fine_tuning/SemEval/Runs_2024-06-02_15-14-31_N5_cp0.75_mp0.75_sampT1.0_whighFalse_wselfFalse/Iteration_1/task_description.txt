Break down CTRs into four distinct components: (1) ELIGIBILITY CRITERIA, which outlines the inclusion and exclusion criteria for trial participants, (2) INTERVENTION, detailing the treatment type, dosage, administration frequency, and treatment duration, (3) RESULTS, summarizing participant demographics, outcome metrics, units of measurement, and study conclusions, and (4) ADVERSE EVENTS, documenting any observed signs and symptoms. Examine and substantiate clinical statements that reference or contrast information from these CTR segments.

CTRs serve as crucial references, supplying comprehensive information on clinical study protocols, outcomes, and safety and efficacy data. Key elements of CTRs encompass:

1. Patient Selection Criteria: Thorough requirements for patient eligibility
2. Intervention Details: Extensive intervention specifications, such as dosages, techniques, and durations
3. Patient Profiles: In-depth patient information, incorporating diagnostic assessments and trial results
4. Adverse Event Documentation: Detailed records of adverse event occurrences, symptoms, and intensities

Clinical experts may consult one or more CTRs for comprehensive evaluations.

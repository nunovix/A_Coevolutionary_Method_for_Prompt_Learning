The Clinical Trial Reports (CTRs) consist of standard sections, which include:
1. CTR Information: This section encompasses the study design, population, interventions, and outcomes data.
2. Description of Data: This subsection outlines the data collection methods, data quality, and data analysis plan in detail.
3. Results: This subsection presents the statistical analyses and the interpretation of the trial's results.
4. Discussion: This subsection interprets the results in the context of the literature and proposes recommendations for future research.
5. Conclusion: This subsection summarizes the main findings and their implications for clinical practice.
6. References: This subsection lists all the sources cited in the report.
